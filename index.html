The Work

MASO is an exploration of sound as a permanent architectural presence. Each work is conceived not as a conventional loudspeaker, but as an artefact designed to exist within the language of architecture.

Materials, proportion and structural expression are treated as carefully as acoustic performance, allowing the object to operate simultaneously as sound system and spatial intervention.

The Author

Guy Traviss is a designer working at the intersection of architecture, sound and material culture. His work explores how technology can be integrated into living environments without visual intrusion.
