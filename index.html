The Author

Guy Traviss is a designer and creative thinker who explores the intersection of sound, architecture, and visual expression. With a background spanning product design, industrial aesthetics, and experiential installations, Guy seeks to create work that is both timeless and evocative.

His projects often explore subtle materiality, careful spatial relationships, and the emotional resonance of everyday environments, with an emphasis on craft, clarity, and enduring quality.
