AUTHOR WORKS
GUY TRAVISS
Guy Traviss is the creator of MASO. A classical guitarist and researcher, his work explores sound, permanence, and architectural presence. Each piece is conceived as both instrument and object — a bridge between performance, space, and material.

SOUND GIVEN PERMANENCE
MASO works are architectural audio objects created in extremely limited numbers. Each piece is designed to integrate with space as both artwork and instrument — not a device but a presence.
