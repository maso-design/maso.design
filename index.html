The Work

MASO develops sculptural audio systems that exist between fine art and architecture. Each piece is conceived as a long-term spatial intervention rather than a replaceable device.

Materials, proportions, and acoustic behaviour are considered together as a single design problem.

The Author

MASO is created by Guy Traviss, whose work explores sound reproduction as an architectural medium.

His background spans audio engineering, design strategy, and spatial thinking, with a focus on objects intended to endure physically and culturally.
